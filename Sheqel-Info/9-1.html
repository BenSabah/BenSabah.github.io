Bibliography and Resources

During the painstaking work of compiling SHEQEL The Online Catalog of Israel Numismatics 1927 - present, a multitude of printed and online resources was consulted. Our extensive numismatic library and online resources enables us to edit, revise and update the catalog on an almost daily basis, making it the world's only comprehensive and up-to-date online Israel numismatic reference source.
